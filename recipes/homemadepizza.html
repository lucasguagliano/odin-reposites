How to make a homemade pizza

Tasty and fast to do? Sure, here's a recipe for homemade pizza

Ingredients
  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp instant yeast
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 ybsp olive oil
  • 1 cup warm water
  • Pizza sauce
  • Cheese
  • Toppings of your choice

Recipe
  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, yeast, salt, and sugar.
  2. Add the olive oil and warm water to the dry ingredients and mix until the dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough for 5-10 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  4. Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl and cover it with plastic wrap. Let it rest for 30 minutes to 1 hour until it doubles in size.
  5. Preheat your oven to 425°F (218°C).
  6. Roll out the dough on a floured surface to the desired thickness.
  7. Place the rolled-out dough on a pizza pan or baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  8. Spread pizza sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  9. Sprinkle cheese over the sauce.
  10. Add your desired toppings.
  11. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and the crust is golden brown.
  12. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
Enjoy your delicious homemade pizza!
