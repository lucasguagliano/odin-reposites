How to make chips at home!

Wanna eat something while watching a serie? Sure, here's a recipe for homemade potatoes chips

Ingredients
  • 2 large potatoes
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Wash and dry the potatoes.
  3. Use a mandoline or sharp knife to slice the potatoes into very thin rounds.
  4. In a large bowl, toss the potato slices with the olive oil and salt until they are evenly coated.
  5. Place the potato slices in a single layer on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  6. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the chips are golden brown and crispy.
  7. Remove the baking sheet from the oven and let the chips cool for a few minutes before serving.
Enjoy your delicious homemade potato chips! You can also experiment with different seasonings and spices to customize the flavor to your liking.
